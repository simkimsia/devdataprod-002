How it works

\[Value = Principal * ( (1 + (rate/n))^(n * t) )\]

Where

  1. rate is the decimal representation of the interest rate. E.g. 3% means 0.03
  2. n is the number of times in a year the interest is compounded
  3. t is the number of years
